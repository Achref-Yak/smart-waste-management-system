Why choose us?

Waste Monitoring solution allows cities and businesses to uncover and understand the complex dynamics of their waste production, using our patented IoT tech site to reduce waste-related costs and save the planet, all at once.

Smart Routing

Trash-Net Route Planning solution enables you to optimize and automate planning of waste collection routes. It combines the Smart Waste Management System, Driver Navigation App and it can incorporate data from Smart Sensors and/or Smart Tags.

Smart Sensors

Trash-Net Smart sensors measure fill-levels in bins via ultrasonic beams. Our sensors can monitor any type of waste (mixed waste, paper, plastics, glass, clothing, bio-waste, liquids, electronics, metal….) in bins and containers of various types and sizes.
We design, develop and produce the sensors and all software in-house and we are proud of it.

Smart Analytics

Collection Efficiency Analysis is one of the analytical tools we have developed to assess the current practices, estimate the possible improvements and follow up on how the deployment of Trash-Net tools improves efficiency across your waste collection activities.
